Saint Joseph School

Addis Ababa

St. Joseph School in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia was established in 1959 at the request of Emperor Haile Silassie I, King of Ethiopia, to educate boys from grades one to 12. St. Joseph’s serves more than 1,600 students in full-day classes with approximately 100 teachers and support staff members. St. Joseph School admits students to first grade at the age of six or seven, and many students graduate at the age of 18.
